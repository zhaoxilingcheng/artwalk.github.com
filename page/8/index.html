Use AppleScript as AutoHotkey On Mac

Posted on 2016-07-26 |

For the good test of Soylent 1.6, I bought 20+ boxes recently.
But there was a problem that Safari does not support AutoFill Form in that website. So I have to input full informations of my credit card manually for the transport fee and the tariff.

All the payment steps are merely number, date, cvv, password.

I was broke down after repeated it again and agin.

It should be automated.

For God’s sake, it occurred to me that the AutoHotkey when I was still using Windows four years ago.

Mac has the AppleScript which can simulate keyboard input and mouse click.

And I found an interesting thing in this process which is the cvv can’t be clicked by the script.

It seems the bank’s website really do something on security.

The side effect of Polyphasic sleep

Posted on 2016-05-17 |

I tried Polyphasic sleep when the first time I heard about it. It’s almost 2 years after that time.

It’s not a rigorous Polyphasic sleep, for I’m using 6 hour major sleep episode and three 30-minute power naps. And I use hypnosis when I have naps at the same time.

It’s really good. Usually I’ll wake up at 6 in the morning and do tons of work, then I feel tired and have the first nap at 10 or 11 and wake up with many Chinese people. I’ll have the second nap after lunch, the third when I went home.

This hack method gives me an illusion that daytime is stretched, because every time I wake up from a nap, I feel it’s totally a new day.

But I found a side effect recently, which is the malaise was also magnified. Especially I’m in anxiety these days.

It’s not polyphasic sleep’s fault, I think, time to read Burns David’s When Panic Attacks.
